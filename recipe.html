Cam's SouthernTossed Omlette

Cam's Delight- Turkey sausage, peppers, with or without a desired side!

My Resume

My Recipe

  • 3 Eggs
  • One Half a Green or Red Pepper (diced)
  • 1lb already cooked diced Turkey Meat
  • 1 Cup of Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 tsp of Salt and pepper

  1. Heat a stovetop skillet on medium to high heat
  2. Once warm, drop a tbsp of vegetable oil (or 1/4 a stick of butter)into the pan.
  3. Doublecheck that the sides of the pan are greased
  4. Crack the 3 large eggs into the pan and stir on low heat
  5. Add your vegetables and diced turkey into the skillet, on top of the egg mixture
  6. Cook for 5 minutes and sprinkle cheese ontop of the mix
  7. "Toss" one Big time and allow 5 minutes for the other side of the egg to cook
  8. Let cool for 1 minute before serving
